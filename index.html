3-Day Spiritual Circuit (Focus: Buddhism & Pilgrimage)

Best for:First-time visitors, short trips

  • Day 1: Arrive Patna -> Drive to Bodh Gaya(=100km, 3hrs). Visit Mahabodhi Temple, Bodhi Tree, Great Buddha Statue.
  • Day 2: Bodh Gaya full day - Explore monasteries (Thai, Japanese, Tibetan), meditate, evening aarti.
  • Day 3: Drive to Rajgir (=80 km) -> Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Hot Springs, Vulture's Peak -> Return to Patna or Gaya airport.

Best time: Oct-Mar | Approx. cost (per person): 8,000-12000 (excluding flights)
